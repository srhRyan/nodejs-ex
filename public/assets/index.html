Angela's Message

Dear Taiwan GBS Team,

I am excited to announce a great news: GBS Taiwan University will be hold on April 11-13 at National Taiwan University. The 3-day University is the best reward to recognize all of your great contribution in 2018. I am looking forward to learn with you and to have a lot of fun together!

“Inspired talent” is one of our commitment to you. Restlessly reinventing ourselves differentiates GBS from the competitors. The University theme is Collaborate to Accelerate and Enchanted to Succeed.

You will learn GBS Taiwan strategy and key message in the Town Hall Meeting on 4/11 morning, get “enchanted” with magic power through 3-day classroom learning, celebrate in Spring Gala on 4/12 evening, and harvest in the Graduation Ceremony on 4/13 evening.

Let's well prepared for this learning carnival in April!

Rules

選課規則

  1. 選課開放時間為 2019/03/25 09:00 - 2019/03/27 17:00。
  2. Town Hall Meeting、Plenary Meeting Session 和 Graduation Ceremony 為必修課，系統已自動加入您的課程清單中。
  3. 請報名 Spring Gala，工作團隊將依名單保留您的席次。
  4. 部分課程由 Manager 提名上課名單，若您被提名，系統將自動加入您的課程清單中。
  5. 課程設有條件限制，不符合條件者，除系統將自動擋修外，L&K 有權利人工退選該門課程。
  6. 同一時段不可選擇一堂以上之課程，系統會自動排除衝堂，如需選擇衝堂課程，需退選後重新選課。
  7. 每人有一次候補機會，可選擇一堂額滿課程。如有人退選將依序候補。候補成功，系統將優先排入候補課（原先已正取之課程會自動取消）。
  8. 每人至少應選擇 24 小時課程（含必修課，不含 Spring Gala），若未選滿系統將於 3/28 自動幫您排課。
  9. 課程確認信與課前作業將於 3/29 陸續發送，系統將以 Event & Class ID 發送行事曆邀請，一切以系統發出邀請為主。
